Thank you for helping improve public-domain book translations. By submitting a pull request, issue suggestion, translation, edit, review, glossary entry, metadata change, cover/layout improvement, or any other contribution to this project, you agree to the rules below.

## Contributions

Contributions may include translations, edits, proofreading, glossary entries, style guidance, notes, metadata, EPUB layout, covers, scripts, automation, issue reports, trial reading feedback, and quality review records.

## Public Release

Your non-code contributions may be included in this project and released to the public under `CC BY-NC-SA 4.0`, as described in [LICENSE.en.md](LICENSE.en.md).

## LifeBook Shufang Use

By contributing, you agree that your contribution may be used by LifeBook Shufang in LifeBook products and services.

LifeBook Shufang is responsible for project organization, editing, publication, quality control, license management, and contributor return arrangements.

Contributors keep attribution interests in their contributions. The specific attribution format, contribution records, and return arrangements are determined and adjusted by LifeBook Shufang based on contribution type, contribution amount, merge status, project stage, and actual operating conditions.

LifeBook Shufang may provide attribution, project benefits, discounts, rewards, revenue sharing, or other forms of return to important contributors according to project rules and actual circumstances. Specific percentages, conditions, and implementation methods are determined by LifeBook Shufang.

## Third-Party Commercial Use

Third-party commercial use of translations, EPUBs, edited content, or other non-code creative material from this project requires separate permission from LifeBook Shufang and the relevant rights holders. The public license does not automatically grant third-party commercial use rights.

## Code Contributions

By contributing code, you agree that the code may be released under the MIT License in [LICENSE.en.md](LICENSE.en.md), unless a file or directory clearly states another license.

## Do Not Submit

Please do not submit modern copyrighted translations, text copied from pirate sites or unclear EPUB downloads, materials you do not have the right to submit, or private credentials and paid resources.
