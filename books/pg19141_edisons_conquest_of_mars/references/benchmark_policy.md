# Benchmark And Reference Translation Policy

This policy applies to every language direction.

本政策适用于所有语言方向。

## Rules / 规则

- Public-domain reference translations may be committed only when their rights status is documented.
- 公版参考译文只有在版权状态已记录时才可以提交到仓库。
- Copyrighted reference translations may be used only as short, lawful, user-provided private evaluation excerpts.
- 受版权保护的参考译文只能作为用户合法提供的短片段私有评估材料使用。
- Do not store long copyrighted source or translation excerpts in the repository.
- 不要在仓库中保存长段受版权保护的原文或译文。
- Benchmark conclusions may be saved; protected text should not be reproduced.
- 可以保存基准测试结论，但不应复刻受保护文本。
- A benchmark should teach method, not force imitation of an unrelated author's style.
- 基准样本应提供方法参照，而不是强迫译文模仿无关作者的腔调。
